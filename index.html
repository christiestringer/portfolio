About

It was fifth form, I was choosing my subjects for the following year, having already chosen French and Accounting, I had one spot available. That’s when Dad suggested I pick up the Computer Science class as I had always “had a knack for computers”. Five years on, all I can say in French is ‘je m'appelle Christie’ and I can probably attempt and fail at creating a balance sheet, but that suggestion from my Dad is something I couldn’t be more grateful for.

Although coding is my passion, I have always had an interest in marketing which led to my decision to pursue a conjoint degree of Computer Science and Marketing. I am currently in my third year of this degree, and have just finished my first summer tech internship with Healthlink, a great software company which taught me so much about the industry and gave me a clear direction of where I want to head.

Front-end development is currently where I want to develop my skills, I want to build more projects surrounding front-end frameworks. However, I hope to one day call myself a full stack developer, as I have interest in all fields of development. I want to continue to develop my skills in an environment which encourages personal growth and a culture that has high involvement and a fun atmosphere.
